# Maintainers

Maintainers use CodeWiki to keep project intent, roadmap state, and implementation evidence close to the repository.

They need CodeWiki to answer:

- what is current,
- what changed,
- what is planned,
- what needs a decision,
- what evidence supports closure.

## Success signals

- Maintainers can inspect project state without reading raw generated files.
- Product and system knowledge stay current rather than historical.
- Roadmap tasks capture active delta instead of burying plans in prose.
- Validation reports make blocked or failed handoffs explicit.
